train.training(img_file_path, annotation_file_path, detector_name)¶

Train SVM classifier given symbol annotations and feature extractor SVM parameters: kernel=’linear’, C=2.67, decision_function_shape= “ovo” trained model will be saved as ”../models/?_svm.pkl”, ? is the feature extraction method.

Parameters:
  • img_file_path (string) – path to score image
  • annotation_file_path (string) – path to annotations
  • detector_name (string) – “hog” or “pixel” – two types of features
